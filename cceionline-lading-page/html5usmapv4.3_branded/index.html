Child Development AssociateÂ® (CDA) Training Certification Program

Our Self-Study CDA programs meet the 120 clock hours of professional development required by The Council for Professional Recognition in order to obtain the National CDA Credential with an Infant/Toddler or Preschool setting. The CDA online training programs are each comprised of eight Instructional Units. These units focus on the six CDA Competency Standards established by The Council for Professional Recognition.

Here are some of the many benefits of CDA training certification programs:

  • High graduation rates
  • Earn the required 120 hours of training in all eight CDA subject areas
  • Web-based coursework accessible 24/7/365
  • Up to one year to complete, or sooner if schedule allows
  • Accessible Education Coaches to provide on-going assistance and support in reaching your goals
  • Support for students in preparing portfolios and other Council requirements toward Credential
  • Programs available in both English and Spanish. In addition, students who complete the program may be able to articulate their training through one of CCElâs articulation partners
