A few words about Kitchen Tribes:

Kitchen Tribes is a dine in, take out and delivery restaurant offering an array of Americana meal items like burgers and fries and chicken strips. As well as, World Fare like pierogies, gyros, stir fries, and Hawaiian plates. Last but not least we have a fresh foods kitchen which includes an assortment of healthy alternatives like salads and Acai bowls. Our ice cream parlor offers soft serve ice cream treats including cones, blenders, milkshakes, ice cream parfaits, and sundaes.

Why choose our restaurant

We have been coming to Kitchen Tribes since they opened. They had a few hiccups along the way. But they are responsive to reviews and always strive to make it right for the customer. The food is delicious! Hot, fresh, and the servings are plentiful. The variety and quality keeps us coming back. The owners are very dedicated to success for their business and satisfying their visitors. Try everything once and you’ll definitely find favorites. Ours are the gyros, gyro bowls and both teriyaki offerings. The egg rolls are awesome! And their new noodle bowls are excellent!

Heather Shoemaker

This was the best food in town that we have ever had by a long shot!!! I love how diverse the menus are so we don’t have to compromise on where to go. There’s something for everyone!!! Portions are big enough that most people can make two meals of it depending on what menu you order from. I had the Greek chicken bowl and it was DELICIOUS!!! Definitely will be making this our go to place to eat in town!!!

Sandra Parks

My family enjoyed our time at Kitchen Tribes. There was something for everyone on the menu and it was delicious! The open air seating was perfect for enjoying the great weather and giving my toddler room to move around while we waited for our order. And there is ice cream! We were visiting Labor Day weekend, so many of the downtown South Haven venues were packed. Kitchen Tribes is great for anybody, but especially for people with kids who just need a good, relaxed place to sit and eat.
