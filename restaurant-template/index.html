A few words about Kitchen Tribes:

Kitchen Tribes is a dine in, take out and delivery restaurant offering an array of Americana meal items like burgers and fries and chicken strips. As well as, World Fare like pierogies, gyros, stir fries, and Hawaiian plates. Last but not least we have a fresh foods kitchen which includes an assortment of healthy alternatives like salads and Acai bowls. Our ice cream parlor offers soft serve ice cream treats including cones, blenders, milkshakes, ice cream parfaits, and sundaes.
